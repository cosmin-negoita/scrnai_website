Is there an option to review the AI’s analysis manually?

Yes, while the AI automatically scores and ranks candidates, you can review the analysis and make manual adjustments if needed.

How long does it take to get results from a screening?

Our AI can process up to 1000 applications per hour, so you can expect results in just a few minutes.

How does the AI ensure unbiased screening?

The AI evaluates specific criteria in the video responses, such as keywords, tone, and nonverbal cues, to assess candidates' suitability for the role.

Is there a way to trial the AI screening feature before committing?

Yes, you can sign up for a free account and try out the AI screening feature with a limited number of applications.

How accurate is the AI in screening candidates?

Our AI is designed to be highly accurate, but the effectiveness can vary depending on the specific criteria and the quality of the applications.
